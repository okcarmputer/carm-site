artist statement


Engaging with any form of media or A(a)rt is almost always monumental in a person's life. I would say personally it can be life altering. My goal with this site is to reinvent and reimagine the feelings and experiences of digesting a form of A(a)rt for the first time. In the situation I leave a movie theater feeling a sense of connection to a character, why should that end with the end credits? It is my goal to learn everything the artist intended to portray through their A(a)rt. I absorb, decipher, and engage with the art in ways that allow me to continue to enjoy and learn from it for longer than the duration of the piece itself.


value statement


Engaging with this site is intended to be a relatable experience. The purpose is to find more ways to engage with your favorite pieces of art, and understand them further. Within a capitalist society it is imperitive that we as humans are intentional about our lives being full, and A(a)rt is a necessity in this area.
